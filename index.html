How it works

The experience is simple. The feeling is not.

1. Scan your photo history

Evercrossed reads time and location metadata from your photo library on your device to build a compact comparison profile.

2. Compare with someone

Share and compare profiles to discover whether your lives overlapped in the same place at the same time.

3. Reveal the hidden moment

Experience the first crossing through a cinematic, emotional reveal you can remember and share.

Built privacy-first

Evercrossed is designed around a simple principle: intimate moments should not require invasive infrastructure.

On-device by design

The app is built to work primarily with data already available on your device, instead of depending on a large backend service.

Metadata, not memories

Evercrossed focuses on photo metadata like time and location rather than the image content itself for its core experience.

Simple and lightweight

No bloated social graph, no endless feed, and no unnecessary complexity. Just one beautiful idea, done carefully.

A different kind of social app

Evercrossed is not about broadcasting your life. It is about discovering the quiet ways life may have already connected it to someone else.

In a world full of noise, Evercrossed makes room for a softer kind of magic: the realization that two stories may have been intertwined long before either person noticed.
