Privacy Policy

Your hidden moments should stay personal.

Evercrossed is designed to help people discover meaningful past crossings while keeping the experience as private and lightweight as possible.

Overview

Evercrossed is a privacy-first mobile app that helps users discover when they may have unknowingly crossed paths with someone in the past.

The app is designed to work primarily using photo metadata already stored on the user’s device, such as timestamps and location information.

What Evercrossed uses

For its core functionality, Evercrossed may use metadata associated with photos, including:

  • Photo timestamps
  • Photo location metadata, if available
  • Locally generated comparison data needed to detect possible crossings

How data is handled

Evercrossed is designed so that its core experience works primarily on-device. The app does not rely on a traditional account system or a large backend service for its main functionality.

Evercrossed does not use photo image content itself as the basis of its core matching experience. Instead, it focuses on metadata such as time and location.

Privacy-first by design

Data collection

Evercrossed is intended to minimize collection of personal data.

If the app does not transmit personal data off-device for the core experience, then your photo metadata remains on your device except where you explicitly choose to share generated comparison information yourself.

If Evercrossed adds analytics, crash reporting, cloud features, or other external services in the future, this policy may be updated accordingly.

User choice

Users control whether to grant the app access to photos and whether to share comparison information with others.

If you do not want Evercrossed to access your photo metadata, you can deny photo permissions or stop using the relevant features.
